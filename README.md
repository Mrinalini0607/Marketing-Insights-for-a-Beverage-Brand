# Marketing-Insights-for-a-Beverage-Brand
Marketing Insights for F&B
A data-driven analytics project that uncovers consumer behavior, marketing effectiveness, and product preferences in the Food & Beverage (F&B) industry.
Overview
This project analyzes consumer survey data to generate valuable marketing insights for an energy drink brand operating in the F&B sector.
It helps businesses understand:
Consumer purchasing behavior
Preferred marketing channels
Brand perception
Packaging preferences
Product development focus areas
Market expansion opportunities
